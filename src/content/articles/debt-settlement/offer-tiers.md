<h2>Tiers</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Tier 1 (≥75% of balance)</strong> — agent-approved.</li>
  <li><strong>Tier 2 (60–74%)</strong> — supervisor approval.</li>
  <li><strong>Tier 3 (&lt;60%)</strong> — director approval, with hardship documentation.</li>
</ul>
