<h2>Where Rehab 1 sits</h2>
<p>Rehab 1 is the first cure cycle after a missed payment. The goal is to cure the account before it advances to Rehab 2 or charge-off.</p>

<h2>Standard cadence</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Day 1–3: courtesy contact attempts (inbound + outbound).</li>
  <li>Day 4–10: structured outreach with payment options.</li>
  <li>Day 11+: review for promotion to Rehab 2.</li>
</ul>

<div class="tip-box">
  <p class="tip-label">Reminder</p>
  <p>Always document every attempt in the account note, even if there's no response.</p>
</div>
