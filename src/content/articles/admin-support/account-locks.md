<h2>When to lock</h2>
<p>Lock an account when there's an active dispute, a confirmed compliance flag, or a request from legal.</p>

<h2>Unlock criteria</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Dispute resolved with documentation.</li>
  <li>Compliance review cleared by manager.</li>
  <li>Legal hold released in writing.</li>
</ul>
