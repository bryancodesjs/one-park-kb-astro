<h2>Manual posting</h2>
<p>Manual posts require a manager approval ticket and a copy of the deposit confirmation. Use the "Manual Post" template.</p>

<h2>Reversals</h2>
<p>Misposted payments must be reversed within the same business day. After cutoff, reversals require a reconciliation ticket.</p>
