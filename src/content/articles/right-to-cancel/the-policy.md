<h2>Window</h2>
<p>Merchants may cancel within the disclosed window from the agreement signing date. Required disclosure language is published in Compliance.</p>

<h2>Process</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Cancellation request must be in writing.</li>
  <li>Funds (if disbursed) reversed within five business days.</li>
  <li>Account closed with note: "Cancelled within window."</li>
</ul>
