<h2>Tiers</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Tier 1 — Team Lead</strong>: standard exceptions, terms inside policy.</li>
  <li><strong>Tier 2 — Manager</strong>: settlement outside Tier 1 limits, ADDS extensions.</li>
  <li><strong>Tier 3 — Director</strong>: legal-adjacent disputes, regulatory mentions, large settlements.</li>
</ul>

<h2>What to send</h2>
<p>Always include the account ID, a one-line summary of what you've already tried, and the specific decision you need.</p>
