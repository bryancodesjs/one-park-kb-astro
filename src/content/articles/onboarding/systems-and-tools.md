<h2>Your daily toolkit</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Salesforce</strong> — CRM. All lead data, call logs, and pipeline management live here. This is your single source of truth.</li>
  <li><strong>RingCentral</strong> — Phone system. Used for all inbound and outbound client calls. Calls are recorded automatically.</li>
  <li><strong>Knowledge Base (this app)</strong> — Your go-to resource for quick answers during calls. Search by keyword or browse by category.</li>
  <li><strong>Slack</strong> — Internal communication. Use it to reach your team lead, funding managers, or compliance when you have a question mid-call that you can't answer yourself.</li>
  <li><strong>DocuSign</strong> — You won't use this directly, but clients will receive agreements via DocuSign. Know that this is how they sign.</li>
</ul>

<div class="tip-box">
  <p class="tip-label">Two-monitor workflow tip</p>
  <p>Keep Salesforce on your primary monitor and the Knowledge Base on your secondary. This lets you reference resources without losing sight of the client's record. Set this up on your first day.</p>
</div>
