<h2>The three must-haves</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>600+ FICO score</strong> — personal credit score of the business owner</li>
  <li><strong>$10,000+ monthly revenue</strong> — consistent monthly sales/revenue from the business</li>
  <li><strong>3+ months in business</strong> — legally registered and actively operating</li>
</ul>

<h2>Additional notes</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Business must be based in the <strong>United States or Puerto Rico</strong></li>
  <li>Must be a <strong>for-profit business</strong></li>
  <li>Personal guarantee is typically required from the primary owner</li>
</ul>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Do not guarantee approval</p>
  <p>These are minimum thresholds, not approval guarantees. Final decisions depend on underwriting review. Never tell a client they are approved before underwriting confirms it.</p>
</div>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Quick qualifier on the call</p>
  <p>"Just to make sure we have options for you — can you tell me roughly what your monthly revenue looks like? And how long have you been in business?"</p>
</div>
