<h2>We fund across virtually every industry</h2>
<p>OPF works with small businesses across a wide range of sectors. If a client asks "Do you fund businesses like mine?" — the answer is almost always yes.</p>

<h2>Common industries we serve</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Restaurants &amp; Food Service</li>
  <li>Retail (brick-and-mortar and e-commerce)</li>
  <li>Construction &amp; Contractors</li>
  <li>Healthcare &amp; Medical Practices</li>
  <li>Auto Repair &amp; Dealerships</li>
  <li>Transportation &amp; Trucking</li>
  <li>Beauty &amp; Personal Care (salons, spas)</li>
  <li>Professional Services (legal, accounting, consulting)</li>
  <li>Cleaning &amp; Janitorial Services</li>
  <li>Landscaping &amp; Lawn Care</li>
  <li>Childcare &amp; Education Services</li>
  <li>Wholesale &amp; Distribution</li>
  <li>Manufacturing (light)</li>
  <li>Home Services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical)</li>
</ul>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Restricted industries</p>
  <p>Some industries may have additional underwriting requirements or may not qualify. If a client mentions cannabis, gambling, adult entertainment, or weapons — loop in your funding manager before continuing.</p>
</div>
