<h2>The 4-step timeline</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Step 1 — Pre-qualify:</strong> Takes just a few minutes on the phone or online. We ask about monthly revenue, time in business, and basic business info.</li>
  <li><strong>Step 2 — Assigned a Funding Manager:</strong> Once pre-qualified, the client is matched with a dedicated funding manager who guides them through the process.</li>
  <li><strong>Step 3 — Review the offer:</strong> Underwriting reviews the application and generates a funding offer. The client reviews terms, factor rate, and total payback.</li>
  <li><strong>Step 4 — Funded:</strong> Once the client accepts the offer, funds can be in their account in as little as <strong>72 hours</strong>.</li>
</ul>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"From the time we finish this call to the time funds hit your account, we're talking as little as 72 hours. No waiting weeks. No back-and-forth with a bank. We move fast because we know your business can't always wait."</p>
</div>
