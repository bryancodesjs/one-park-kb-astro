<h2>The soft close</h2>
<p>Never end a call without a clear next action — agreed to by the client. Even if they're not ready to accept an offer today, there should be a specific follow-up commitment.</p>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">After presenting the offer</p>
  <p>"Based on everything we've talked about — does this sound like something you'd like to move forward with today?"</p>
</div>

<h2>If yes — launch the process</h2>
<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"Perfect. I'm going to transfer you to your dedicated funding manager right now. They'll walk you through the next steps and get the documentation process started. You could have funds in your account in as little as 72 hours."</p>
</div>

<h2>If they need more time</h2>
<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"No problem. I'll hold your information and we can reconnect when you're ready. Can we schedule a specific time — how does [tomorrow at 2pm] work?"</p>
</div>

<div class="tip-box">
  <p class="tip-label">Always log the outcome</p>
  <p>Immediately after the call, update Salesforce with the call disposition, next steps, and any notes about objections or client-specific context. Your funding manager depends on this to pick up where you left off.</p>
</div>
