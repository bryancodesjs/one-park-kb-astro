<h2>Eligibility</h2>
<p>Non-recourse advances are flagged at funding. You'll see a "non-recourse" tag on the account header.</p>

<h2>Effect on collections</h2>
<ul>
  <li>No personal guarantee enforcement.</li>
  <li>Reconciliation requests handled differently — see Reconciliation policy.</li>
  <li>Legal escalation requires senior approval.</li>
</ul>
