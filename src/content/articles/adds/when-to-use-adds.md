<h2>Triggers</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Two consecutive NSF returns.</li>
  <li>Merchant request with hardship documentation.</li>
  <li>Disputed balance pending review.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Approval</h2>
<p>ADDS requires supervisor approval. The suspension lasts up to 7 calendar days unless extended in writing.</p>
