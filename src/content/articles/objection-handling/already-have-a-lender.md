<h2>Don't concede — explore</h2>
<p>Having a current lender doesn't mean they're fully funded or satisfied. Ask questions before accepting this as a closed door.</p>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"That's great — how's that working out for you? Are you fully covered for what you need right now, or is there a gap?"</p>
</div>

<h2>If there's a gap — stacking may apply</h2>
<p>Depending on their existing balance and our underwriting guidelines, a client may qualify for additional capital stacked on top of their current position. Always loop in your funding manager before representing this as an option.</p>

<h2>If they're fully funded</h2>
<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"Totally understand — I'm glad you're covered. When does your current position renew? I'd love to stay in touch so we can be on your radar when the time comes. A lot of our clients come back to us for their second and third rounds because of how smooth the process is."</p>
</div>

<div class="tip-box">
  <p class="tip-label">Pro tip</p>
  <p>Log the renewal date in Salesforce and set a follow-up task 30 days before their expected payoff. Being proactive before they go searching again is how you win the next round.</p>
</div>
