<h2>Validate first</h2>
<p>Getting declined by a bank is frustrating and demoralizing. Start by acknowledging that experience before pivoting to what's different about OPF.</p>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Say this</p>
  <p>"I'm really sorry that happened — and unfortunately it's more common than it should be. Traditional banks turn down a huge percentage of small business applications, often for reasons that have nothing to do with how well the business is actually doing."</p>
</div>

<h2>Explain what's different</h2>
<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Continue with</p>
  <p>"What we look at is different. We focus on your monthly revenue and business health — not just your credit score. If your business is generating $10,000 or more per month and you've been operating for at least 3 months, we have options for you."</p>
</div>

<h2>OPF's minimum requirements (quick reference)</h2>
<ul>
  <li>600+ FICO score</li>
  <li>$10,000+ in monthly revenue</li>
  <li>3+ months in business (legally registered)</li>
  <li>Business located in the US or Puerto Rico</li>
</ul>

<div class="tip-box">
  <p class="tip-label">Pro tip</p>
  <p>Ask "What was the reason they gave you?" — this helps you identify whether the client truly doesn't qualify or whether it was a bank-specific reason (e.g., no business credit history, industry restriction) that wouldn't apply to us.</p>
</div>
