<h2>Required docs</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Three months of merchant processing statements.</li>
  <li>Bank statements covering the same period.</li>
  <li>Signed reconciliation request form.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Turn-around</h2>
<p>Standard review is 5 business days. Communicate the timeline at the moment of request — don't promise faster.</p>
