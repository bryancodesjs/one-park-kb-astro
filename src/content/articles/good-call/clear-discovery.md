<h2>Why this is a good call</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Used the merchant's name three times in the first 30 seconds.</li>
  <li>Asked open-ended discovery before offering options.</li>
  <li>Closed with a two-option choice instead of an open prompt.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Listen for</h2>
<p>Pacing slows in the middle so the merchant can respond. The agent doesn't fill silence with new offers — they let the merchant decide.</p>
