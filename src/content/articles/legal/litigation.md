<h2>Comms freeze</h2>
<p>Once an account enters active litigation, all merchant outreach must route through legal. Direct contact is prohibited.</p>

<h2>Internal updates</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Weekly status review with the legal partner.</li>
  <li>Document every notice received and forward to legal within one business day.</li>
</ul>
