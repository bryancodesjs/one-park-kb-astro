<h2>Eligibility</h2>
<p>Garnishment requires a finalized judgment plus a verified bank or income source. State limits override our internal targets.</p>

<h2>Workflow</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Confirm judgment is recorded.</li>
  <li>Submit garnishment request to legal partner.</li>
  <li>Track service of process and respond to objections.</li>
</ul>
