<h2>When we file</h2>
<p>Judgment is sought after exhaustion of Rehab 1, Rehab 2, and at least one settlement attempt — with full documentation.</p>

<h2>Pre-filing checklist</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Signed agreement on file.</li>
  <li>Complete payment history.</li>
  <li>Most recent merchant communication log.</li>
  <li>Notice-of-intent letter sent and acknowledged or returned.</li>
</ul>
