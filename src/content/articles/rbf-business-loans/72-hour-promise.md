<h2>What "72 hours" really means</h2>
<p>Once a client accepts their offer and all required documents are submitted, funds can be deposited into their business bank account in as little as 72 business hours. This clock starts from offer acceptance — not from the first call.</p>

<h2>What the client needs to do to keep it moving</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Respond promptly to document requests from their funding manager</li>
  <li>Provide accurate bank statements (typically 3–6 months)</li>
  <li>Ensure their business bank account is active and accessible for ACH</li>
  <li>Sign the agreement electronically (DocuSign)</li>
</ul>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Common delays</p>
  <p>The biggest cause of delays is slow document submission. If a client wants their money in 72 hours, they need to be responsive. Let them know speed is a two-way street.</p>
</div>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Set expectations on the call</p>
  <p>"We move fast on our end — our goal is to have an offer in front of you quickly. The key is that when your funding manager reaches out for documents, you respond the same day. The faster you move, the faster funds hit your account."</p>
</div>
