<h2>General range</h2>
<p>OPF's funding amounts generally range from <strong>$5,000 to $500,000+</strong>, though the actual offer depends on underwriting review of the client's specific financials.</p>

<h2>How amounts are determined</h2>
<p>Underwriting considers several factors:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Average monthly gross revenue (primary driver)</li>
  <li>Time in business</li>
  <li>Credit profile</li>
  <li>Industry and business type</li>
  <li>Existing debt obligations</li>
  <li>Bank account health (consistency, no chronic overdrafts)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Rough rule of thumb</h2>
<p>Clients can often qualify for <strong>50–150% of their average monthly revenue</strong>. A business doing $30,000/month might see offers between $15,000–$45,000 at the start of the relationship. Higher amounts become available with a successful funding history.</p>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Don't promise specific amounts</p>
  <p>Never tell a client they qualify for a specific dollar amount before underwriting reviews their file. You can share ranges, but final offers come from underwriting only.</p>
</div>
