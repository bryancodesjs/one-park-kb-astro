<h2>Required disclosure</h2>
<p>All calls must include the recording disclosure before any substantive business conversation takes place.</p>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Required language</p>
  <p>"I want to let you know that this call may be recorded for quality and training purposes. Is that okay with you?"</p>
</div>

<h2>When to deliver it</h2>
<p>Immediately after confirming the client can talk — before you begin discovery or discuss any product details.</p>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Do not skip this</p>
  <p>Skipping the recording disclosure is a compliance violation and may result in coaching or corrective action. It must be delivered on every call, every time.</p>
</div>

<h2>If the client objects to being recorded</h2>
<p>If a client refuses to be recorded, stop the call and escalate to your team lead immediately. Do not proceed with a sales conversation.</p>
