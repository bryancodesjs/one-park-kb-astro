<h2>Never say these things</h2>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Guaranteed approval</p>
  <p>Do NOT say: "You're approved," "You'll definitely qualify," or "We can guarantee you funding." Final approval comes from underwriting only. You can say "You may pre-qualify" or "Based on what you've told me, you look like a strong candidate."</p>
</div>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">APR quotes</p>
  <p>Do NOT quote or calculate an APR equivalent for our factor-rate products. These are different financial instruments and an APR comparison is misleading. Only discuss factor rates and total payback amounts.</p>
</div>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Specific funding amounts before underwriting</p>
  <p>Do NOT tell a client they will receive a specific amount (e.g., "You'll get $75,000") before an offer has been generated by underwriting. Share ranges, not promises.</p>
</div>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">False competitor claims</p>
  <p>Do NOT make false or unverifiable negative statements about any competitor. Comparative statements must be factually accurate. When in doubt, don't make the comparison.</p>
</div>

<div class="warning-box">
  <p class="warning-label">Unauthorized use of "loan"</p>
  <p>RBF products should not be described as "loans" in most contexts. Use "funding," "capital," "revenue-based financing," or "merchant cash advance" as appropriate for the product.</p>
</div>

<h2>If you're unsure</h2>
<p>When in doubt, don't say it. Escalate to your team lead or compliance contact before making any representation you're not 100% sure about.</p>
