<h2>What qualifies as a new PA</h2>
<p>A new payment arrangement (New PA) is created the first time a charged-off account agrees to a structured repayment plan after charge-off.</p>

<h2>Required steps</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Verify identity and right party contact.</li>
  <li>Confirm balance and terms in writing.</li>
  <li>Set the first debit date no more than 14 days out.</li>
  <li>Document the PA in the account note with effective date.</li>
</ul>

<div class="script-box">
  <p class="script-label">Setting expectations</p>
  <p>"This arrangement keeps your account out of further legal review as long as the agreed payments come through. Missed payments revert the account to active charge-off status."</p>
</div>
