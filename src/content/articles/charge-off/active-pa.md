<h2>Daily handling</h2>
<p>An Active PA means the account already has a working repayment schedule. Your job is to keep it in flight.</p>

<h2>What to watch</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Missed debit attempt — outreach within 24 hours.</li>
  <li>Bank-level NSF — flag for reconciliation review.</li>
  <li>Merchant request to modify terms — escalate per Escalation Path.</li>
</ul>
